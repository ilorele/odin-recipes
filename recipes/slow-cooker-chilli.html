Slow Cooker Chilli

What is slow cooker chilli

This slow cooker chili is packed with beans, veggies, and spices. The crockpot is a great way to make this beefy chili.

Ingredients:

  • Groung Beef
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Green bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomato puree
  • Canned kidney beans
  • chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Ground black pepper
  • Hot pepper sauce

Steps:

  1. Place beef in a skillet over medium heat and cook until evenly brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain grease.
  2. Transfer beef to a slow cooker and mix in onion, celery, bell pepper, garlic, tomato puree, kidney beans, and cannellini beans. Season with chili powder, salt, basil, oregano, parsley, black pepper, and hot pepper sauce.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours.
